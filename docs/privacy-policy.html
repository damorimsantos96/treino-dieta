Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Treino & Dieta App

This Privacy Policy describes how Treino & Dieta ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our personal fitness and nutrition tracking application.

1. Who We Are

Treino & Dieta is a personal fitness tracking application developed and operated by Diego S. (d.amorim.santos96@gmail.com). The App is intended for personal use.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: email address used to create your account.
  • Health & fitness data: body weight, workout sessions, caloric intake, heart rate, workout duration, and related training metrics that you manually enter into the App.
  • Wearable device data (Whoop): with your explicit authorization, we access workout strain, recovery scores, sleep data, and calorie data from your Whoop account via the official Whoop API.
  • GPS/activity data (Garmin): with your explicit authorization, we access running activity data including distance, pace, heart rate, and duration from your Garmin Connect account.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your daily fitness and nutrition dashboard.
  • Calculate personalized metrics (caloric expenditure, hydration needs, macronutrient targets).
  • Track progress over time through charts and trend analysis.
  • Synchronize data between your wearable devices and the App.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — cloud database and authentication provider. Your data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure. See their privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.
  • Whoop API — we access Whoop health data only with your explicit OAuth authorization. You can revoke access at any time via your Whoop account settings. See whoop.com/privacy-policy.
  • Garmin Connect — activity data is fetched from your Garmin account using credentials you provide. See garmin.com privacy policy.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in a private Supabase database with Row-Level Security (RLS) enabled, meaning only your authenticated account can access your data. Credentials are encrypted at rest. OAuth tokens for Whoop are stored securely and never exposed to the client application.

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you maintain an active account. You may request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Revoke third-party integrations (Whoop, Garmin) at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
